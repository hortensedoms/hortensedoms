# Hortense Doms

Biostatistician with a PhD in Biostatistics (2020–2026), specializing in statistical methodology for complex longitudinal and survival data.

My research focuses on **joint modelling of longitudinal outcomes and time-to-event data**, with a particular emphasis on **quality of life outcomes in cancer clinical trials**.

## 🎓 PhD Thesis

**Joint Modelling of Complex Longitudinal Outcomes and Time-to-Event Data**
*With a Focus on Quality of Life Outcomes in Cancer Clinical Trials*  

Supervisors: Catherine Legrand and Philippe Lambert
